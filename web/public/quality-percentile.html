Belief about a paper's eventual percentile

At submission, with no information, every percentile is equally likely (a flat belief). Review effort and time concentrate the belief toward the paper's true eventual percentile. A tier is earned when we are 90% confident the percentile clears its threshold.

R, review effort0.30
Sharpens the belief; reveals some of the eventual percentile.
t, time0.30
Reveals impact as it accrues; the dominant source of information.

Bands follow the NSF / Leiden percentile classes (top 1 / 5 / 10 / 25 / 50%). Glänzel's parameter-free Characteristic Scores and Scales is an alternative. Belief modelled as a Beta on [0,1], flat at the prior and concentrating with evidence; illustrative.
