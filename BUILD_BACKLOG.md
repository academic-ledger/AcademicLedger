## Decisions log (so they are not relitigated)
- **Official reference class = the synthetic field** â€” a recency-weighted topic-mixture blend of full (subfield, vintage) cohorts (reference-based â†’ community migration). The cited-only co-citation *neighborhood* is **not** a ranking cohort (it's popularity-biased and has no uncited papers); it only informs the topic mixture. (QaL_spec Â§3/Â§5.)
- **Denominator rule:** every percentile is over the **full population** (cited + uncited), never cited-only and never a top-N/sorted sample; the uncited atom takes the **mid-rank 100Â·pâ‚€/2**; the same convention is used for r_obs and râˆž.
